# Lyrics Reference Audio

- Use `tools.audio.inspect_audio` output only for broad lyric constraints such as pacing, density, and likely phrase length.
- Do not claim to hear words, melody, language, singer identity, or copyrighted lyrics from metadata alone.
- If a preview clip exists, treat it as a reference asset for downstream human review; do not quote or imitate it.
- Keep hook text original, short, and aligned with the creative brief.
- When audio duration is short, produce compact verse/chorus lines that fit a short-form song.
